# Canada Warehouse Job Watcher Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026

Canada Warehouse Job Watcher helps users monitor supported Canadian warehouse and shift-job employer career pages, receive browser alerts for matching opportunities, and prepare application pages for user review.

## Information Stored By The Extension

The extension may store the following information locally in the user's browser:

- selected employers
- selected cities and location preferences
- selected shift and job type preferences
- recent detected job matches
- application-assist profile details entered by the user, such as name, phone, address, work eligibility, resume text, and related application details
- subscription status and billing email used to restore access

This information is used only to provide job watching, alerts, subscription access, and application-assist features.

## Information Sent To The Backend

The extension may contact the subscription backend to check whether a billing email has an active subscription and to open Stripe's customer portal for subscription management or cancellation.

The extension may also contact the alert backend when external alerts are enabled. Browser alerts work locally through Chrome notifications.

## Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. The extension does not collect or store credit card numbers or payment card details. Stripe may process payment information according to Stripe's own privacy policy.

## Amazon Live Mode

When the user enables Amazon Live Mode and is logged in on Amazon Hiring Canada, the extension may detect Amazon Hiring authorization requests so it can check available Amazon Canada shifts for the user's selected preferences. This is used only for the Amazon Live Mode feature.

## Application Assist

Application-assist features are user controlled. The extension may fill visible form fields using information the user saved in the extension. The extension is designed to stop before final submit so the user can review and submit applications themselves.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. We do not use user data for creditworthiness, lending, advertising profiling, or unrelated purposes. Data is used only to provide the extension's job watching, alerts, subscription access, and application-assist features.

## Data Retention And Deletion

Users can clear locally stored extension data by removing the extension or clearing the extension's saved data in the browser. Subscription records are managed through Stripe and the billing backend.

## Contact

For privacy questions or support, contact the publisher through the support contact listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.
